Outdoor Gear Checklist

Ionic // AngularJS // Firebase NoSQL // Mobile

A mobile app designed to create, maintain, and optimize outdoor gear loadouts. The primary functionality offers users the ability to select backpacking/hiking items that they currently own or plan to purchase, create a loadout with those items, and then optimize that loadout for certain factors (weight, price, etc.). Users will also be able to share their loadouts and view the loadouts created by other members of the community.

Design Phase

During January 2018, the dev team met to decide on some design for the application. None of us were UI/UX expert, or had developed a Mobile App before. We started with some simple drawings that had the Ionic Framework components in mind. This would allow us to quickly get the front-end set up and avoid wasting too much time.
